Problem & Opportunity

The Problem

Users lack native calendar integration, forcing manual deadline tracking across multiple tools. This causes missed deadlines, duplicated effort, and constant context-switching.

The Opportunity

Calendar integration is the #1 user request. It positions TaskFlow competitively vs Jira and Asana, improves retention, and drives enterprise adoption.

85%

of surveyed users rated calendar integration as a high-priority need (4-5/5)
